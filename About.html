About Me

Hello and Welcome to My Photography Blog, I am a San Antonio based Photographer (Hobby) that enjoys capturing landscapes, sunrises, sunsets and plenty of street photographs. Photography allows me to capture all the little moments in time. It all started with a Photojournalism class in High School and I’ve been hooked ever since. Photography always gives me new opportunities to travel, while finding hidden gems to and from my destination. I enjoy creating unique ways to capture the moment with my camera. I can’t wait to look through the lens of my next adventure.
